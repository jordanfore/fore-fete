About FORE fête

FORE fête is a comprehensive guide to Paris's Fête de la Musique 2026, curated for emerging creatives, independent thinkers, and community builders.

What's inside:

  • 92 events across Paris - club nights, day parties, street activations, boat parties
  • 20 restaurants - street food & indie gems for the young creative crowd
  • Language guide - essential phrases & cultural slang to navigate & engage with locals
  • Interactive map - explore locations, filter by type, discover neighborhoods

We are FORE. We don't wait fore change - we make it. This guide exists for the people, by the people. Where they see barriers, we see opportunity.
